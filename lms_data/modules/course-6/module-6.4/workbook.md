### Exercise 3: Observer Positioning

**Scenario**: Design observer coverage for a laser show with the following parameters:
- Location: Open field, flat terrain
- Audience: 5,000 people in designated area
- Laser projectors: 6 units on stage
- Airspace: Near small airport (Class D), 3 NM away

**Task**: 
1. Calculate minimum number of observers needed
2. Draw observer positions on the site diagram
3. Define communication protocols

### Exercise 5: Integration Exercise - "It's Showtime"

**Full Scenario**: 
You are 45 minutes from showtime. All checks are complete. Suddenly:
- Wind increases from calm to 22 mph sustained
- Weather radar shows storm cells 15 miles away, moving toward venue at 20 mph
- One observer calls in sick - you are short-staffed
- Festival promoter is pressuring you to start on time

**Task**: Develop your response plan addressing:
1. Weather assessment and decision
2. Observer redeployment strategy
3. Communication with promoter
4. Contingency plans (delay, modify, or cancel)
5. Documentation requirements
