☀️ UV-C Radiation (100-280 nm)

Absorption: Completely absorbed by cornea within 0.1 mm

Primary Effect: Photokeratitis ("welder's flash")

Symptoms: Severe pain, photophobia, tearing (delayed 6-12 hours)

Recovery: Usually complete within 24-48 hours

⚠️ Protection Required: UV-blocking eyewear essential

📋 Assessment for Type I

Lower Risk Higher Risk
Characteristics:

Very fair skin, red or blonde hair, freckles

Laser Hazard Considerations:
  • Highest risk for UV-induced damage
  • Minimal melanin protection
  • Requires maximum PPE for UV lasers
  • Visible/IR lasers: Lower risk than darker skin types
Recommended Precautions:

Full coverage clothing, high-SPF sunscreen under PPE, frequent breaks from UV exposure, mandatory protective gloves
