## Module 4.3: Illinois & Massachusetts Regulations

**Location:** `module-4.3/`

**Duration:** 45 minutes

**Difficulty:** Intermediate

### Content Coverage
- **Illinois:** 32 ILCS Part 315 (Laser System Act of 1997)
  - Registration requirements (installation & annual)
  - LSO qualifications (9 specific areas)
  - Entertainment laser requirements (10-day notice)
  - Incident reporting timelines
  - Height requirements for unattended lasers

- **Massachusetts:** 105 CMR 121.000
  - ANSI Z136 series incorporation
  - Entertainment laser provisions
  - $500 maximum civil penalty structure
  - Waiver provisions for undue hardship
  - 15-day vendor notification requirement

### Interactive Components
1. **State Comparison Matrix** - Side-by-side regulatory comparison
2. **Height Requirement Visualizer** - Interactive measurement comparison (IL: 6m vs MA: 20ft)
3. **Notification Calculator** - Date calculator for 10-working-day requirements
4. **LSO Qualification Tracker** - Checklist for 9 IL qualification areas
5. **Penalty Comparison Chart** - Visual enforcement comparison
6. **Tour Planning Worksheet** - Multi-state compliance checklist generator
7. **Branching Scenario** - "Touring Show Compliance Challenge"

  - Class A Certificate (low intensity lasers)
