🎓 Course Learning Objectives

👁️ Identify eye structures vulnerable to laser radiation and explain the focusing effect (100,000× concentration)
🌈 Map wavelength bands to affected ocular structures (retinal hazard region 400-1400 nm)
🔥 Distinguish between thermal, photochemical, and mechanical damage mechanisms
⚠️ Recognize why near-IR (700-1400 nm) is called the "silent killer" of laser safety
🎨 Apply Fitzpatrick skin type scale to assess individual laser hazard risk
🛡️ Select appropriate PPE based on wavelength, power, and exposure conditions
