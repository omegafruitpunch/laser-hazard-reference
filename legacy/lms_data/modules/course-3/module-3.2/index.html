💜 Photochemical Damage

Blue light (400-550 nm): High-energy photons trigger chemical reactions. Accumulates over time. Affects photoreceptors and retinal pigment epithelium.

🔥 Thermal Damage

All wavelengths 400-1400 nm: Laser energy converts to heat. Temperature rise >10°C denatures proteins. Immediate tissue destruction.
